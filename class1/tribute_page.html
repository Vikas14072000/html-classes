shaheed Bhagat Singh


(27 September 1907- 23 March 1931)

Bhagat Singh was a prominent Indian socialist revolutionary whose acts of dramatic defiance against the British Raj made him a national hero. He is best known for his involvement in the Saunders murder and the Central Legislative Assembly bombing, as well as his hunger strike for better conditions for Indian political prisoners.
